Breaking News: Spring Has Sprung

What comes after a grueling semester?

After a grueling semester, student who was suffering through burnout manage to get through it. Lessons to be learnt are things such as taking care of yourself, start homework early, don't try to do everything at once, and take breaks. Make sure to also get enough sleep and eat well. By following these tips next time around, students MIGHT just be able to survive.

Despite the usual standard, spring in the year 2024 has been weird. After raising the expectations of people who hated snow, the season has been surprising everyone with how late it has been dropping snow. Just as people have started in getting out their spring and summer closet, the weather then drops to negative temperatures.

Make sure you heal by sleeping as much as you can. Then start planning your summer. Take care of yourself first! Go find work though or do whatever, as long as you are enjoying yourself.
